Composite score = 50%×GMV/1k + 30%×Orders/1k + 20%×Weighted CTR (all percentile scores). Only creators with videos ≥ 2 and impressions ≥ 10,000 are marked as eligible, but all creators are shown below.

Methodology

  • Weighted CTR (creator): averages each video’s CTR by its impressions. Formula: sum(CTR × Impressions) ÷ sum(Impressions). Treat CTR as a decimal (e.g., 2.5% → 0.025).
  • Estimated Clicks: Impressions × CTR (CTR in decimal).
  • CVR: Orders ÷ Estimated Clicks.
  • AOV: GMV ÷ Orders.
  • Composite priority score: 50%×GMV/1k + 30%×Orders/1k + 20%×Weighted CTR (all percentile ranks).
  • Eligibility threshold: creators with videos ≥ 2 and impressions ≥ 10,000 are marked as eligible.
  • Video ranking thresholds: GMV/1k uses videos with Impr ≥ 1,000; CVR uses videos with Clicks ≥ 30.
